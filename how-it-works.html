Stage 01

Discovery — cast a wide net, intelligently

Three discovery engines run in parallel each cycle:

  • Hashtag discovery — 1,740 long-tail India hashtags (city × category × suffix). 30 tags rotated per round across 3+ passes.
  • Hub account crawling — 133 seed accounts in 5 clusters (bridal, jewellery, ethnic, street, luxury). 4 hubs per cluster per round.
  • 2-hop follower graph — Expands from fashion-signal accounts to their followers who also show selling patterns.
  • Google Maps cross-ref — Finds Instagram handles linked to local business listings by city and category.

Typical output: 2,000–3,000 raw handles per pipeline run

Stage 02

Scraping — enrich every profile

Apify-powered Instagram scraping with smart throttling:

  • Bio pre-check — Lightweight username/bio gate before expensive full post scrape
  • Profile scrape — Followers, post count, bio, business category, verification status
  • Post analysis — Last 10 posts: avg likes, comments, views, posting frequency, last post date
  • Contact extraction — WhatsApp links, emails, external URLs, link-in-bio services

Typical output: 800–1,200 enriched profiles

Stage 03

Analysis — filter noise, detect intent

  • Strict India filter — Bio, hashtags, or location must confirm India market fit
  • Follower threshold — Minimum 5,000 followers for fashion vertical (configurable per industry)
  • Selling intent detection — WhatsApp ordering, Shopify links, "DM to order" captions, price mentions
  • Automation stack analysis — Detects Shopify, Dukaan, Linktree, Google Forms, manual workflows
  • Gate 2 rejection — Memes, fan pages, news, influencers, personal accounts permanently discarded

Typical output: 300–500 qualified businesses

Stage 04

Scoring — rank by buying probability

Composite ranking formula:

  • Activity score (70%) — Engagement rate, views, posting frequency, recency
  • Engagement quality (15%) — Comment-to-like ratio, view consistency
  • Selling intent (15%) — WhatsApp links, DM-to-order, conversion signal strength
  • Buyer fit label — Strong Buyer / Possible Buyer / Weak Buyer / Not a Buyer
  • Lead score adjustments — No website (+22), manual ordering (+18), WhatsApp-only sales (+8)

Export gate: activity score ≥70, HIGH confidence only

Stage 05

Export — deliver ranked, actionable leads

  • Top 100 daily feed — Ranked 1–100 with top 10 highlighted
  • Rich data per lead — Username, platform, category, followers, engagement rate, activity score, conversion signals, profile URL
  • Multi-format — Dashboard, CSV, Excel, Google Sheets (dated tabs)
  • Funnel report — Full conversion metrics from discovery to exportable buyers

Final output: 100 HIGH-confidence ranked leads ready for outreach
