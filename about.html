About

Lead intelligence for the social commerce era

CatalogHub was built to solve one problem: businesses waste time and money on unqualified leads while the best prospects are actively selling on Instagram every day.

Our mission

India's social commerce market is growing from $29.3B to $143.9B by 2030. Millions of businesses sell through Instagram and WhatsApp — but finding them is manual, slow, and unreliable. CatalogHub automates discovery, qualification, and scoring so sales teams focus on closing, not searching.

What we built

Two production-grade engines power the platform:

  • Fashion Social Scraper — Python pipeline with 1,740 hashtags, 133 hub accounts, 2-hop graph discovery, and daily top-100 ranked feeds to Google Sheets.
  • Prospect Engine — TypeScript pipeline with Google Maps + Instagram discovery, dual qualification gates, automation detection, lead scoring, and CSV/Excel export.

Why we're different

We don't sell data. We sell intelligence. Every lead passes through business qualification (rejecting memes, influencers, and personal accounts), buyer qualification (identifying Strong Buyers with manual ordering workflows), and composite scoring (ranking by activity, engagement, and selling intent). Only HIGH-confidence, top-ranked accounts reach your dashboard.

The team

Built by engineers who've run prospect pipelines across Jaipur, Mumbai, Surat, and Delhi — scraping thousands of profiles, qualifying hundreds of Strong Buyers, and closing deals with the same data we now deliver to customers.
